The Aus4ASEAN Futures program is a strategic partnership developed to bolster the three community pillars of ASEAN: Economic, Socio-Cultural, and Political-Security. Managed by a dedicated Program Management Team and the Program Planning and Monitoring Support Unit (PPMSU) within the ASEAN Secretariat, the initiative enhances ASEAN’s responsiveness and alignment with member states’ priorities.
